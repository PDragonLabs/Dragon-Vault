😂 Morning Laughter Yoga (10 min)

  1. Warm-up: Clap + "Ho ho ha ha ha" for 1 minute
  2. One Meter Laugh: Slide hand across arm → big laugh (3x each side)
  3. Lion Laugh: Tongue out, roar, then laugh (5x)
  4. Aloha Laugh: Arms up + "Aloooo..." then laugh (4x)
  5. Free Laughter: Laugh however you want for 2 minutes
  6. Close: Deep breaths + "I am happy, I am healthy"

Tip: Fake it till you make it — real laughter usually kicks in within 30–60 seconds.
